Hard Water is a Growing Problem Across India

Most Indian households silently battle hard water every single day — creating constant demand for an affordable, effective solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can become a D'Cal dealer?

Hardware shops, plumbing stores, sanitary ware dealers, building material suppliers, water purification dealers, home improvement stores and bathroom accessory retailers are all ideal partners.

How much investment is required?

Investment is flexible and depends on your business size and territory. Our team will share dealer pricing and starter packages once you apply.

Do you provide dealer support?

Yes. We provide dedicated dealer and distributor assistance including product guidance, demand support and ongoing supply.

Can I become a distributor?

Absolutely. Distributor opportunities are available for partners looking to supply multiple retailers across a region.

Is dealership available across India?

Yes — this is a Pan-India opportunity. Territories are allotted based on availability, so applying early helps secure your region.

How quickly can I get started?

Once your application is verified and the discussion is complete, you can receive stock and start selling quickly — often within days.
