    Frequently asked
    How long does shipping take? +
    Orders ship within 24 hours and arrive in 3-5 business days across India via DTDC and Bluedart.
    Is installation included? +
    Yes. Our certified plumber installs free within 100km of metro cities. DIY guide included for remote pin codes.
    What's the warranty? +
    Lifetime warranty on the cartridge body. Refill every 12 months.
    Will it work on borewell water? +
    Yes. Tested up to 1200 TDS. We recommend a sediment pre-filter for borewell water.
    How often do I replace the cartridge? +
    One cartridge lasts about 12 months or 50,000 litres for an average family of four. Replacement refills are available on this site and ship within 24 hours.
    Does it remove the minerals my body needs? +
    No. D'Cal conditions the water to stop scale build-up without stripping essential minerals, so the water stays safe and healthy for daily use.
    Will it fit my overhead or underground tank? +
    Yes. The drop-in design fits any standard overhead, underground or storage tank. No drilling, plumbing changes or electricity required.
    What if I'm not satisfied? +
    You're covered by our 7-day hassle-free return policy. If you don't see a difference in scaling, just reach out and we'll arrange a full refund.
