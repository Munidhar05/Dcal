Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about how we ship your order and our return policy.

Shipping

  • Free shipping on orders over ₹2,000 (use code DCAL10 at checkout).
  • Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days.
  • Standard delivery across India takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.
  • You'll receive tracking details once your order ships. Track anytime from Track Order.

Returns & Refunds

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery for unused products in original packaging.
  • If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 48 hours for a free replacement.
  • Approved refunds are credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • You can cancel an order any time before it ships from My Orders.
