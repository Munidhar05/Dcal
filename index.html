    THE HARD TRUTH

    You've spent lakhs on your dream house — don't let hard water spoil it

    82% of Indian homes have hard water. Here's what it's silently destroying every day.

    Hair Fall & Damage

    Calcium binds to hair, strips natural oils, and clogs follicles. Result: chronic hair fall, dry brittle strands, and scalp irritation that no shampoo can fix.

    Rough Dry Skin

    Hard water leaves a mineral film on your skin that disrupts moisture barrier. Result: dryness, eczema flare-ups, premature aging, and cracking.

    Corroded Pipes

    Hard water eats away at your home's plumbing from inside. Iron and rust accumulate, water pressure drops, and pipes need replacement years before their time.

    Stained Sinks

    Yellow, orange, and brown stains lock into ceramic and stainless steel. No amount of regular cleaning removes them — they're mineral deposits embedded in the surface.

    Scaled Tiles

    White limescale film covers your bathroom tiles, glass shower doors, and mirrors. It dulls the shine and looks dirty no matter how often you clean.

    Damaged Heater Rod

    Your geyser's heating coil becomes encrusted with limescale. Efficiency drops 40% in 18 months, electricity bills spike, and the heater dies years early.

    Clogged Faucets

    Mineral deposits choke the aerators in your taps. Water pressure drops, spray patterns get uneven, and chrome finishes pit and peel.

    Weak Showers

    Showerheads clog with calcium scale within months. Spray weakens, water sprays sideways, and pressure drops to a trickle.

    Repairs of all this damage could cost around ₹5 lakhs
